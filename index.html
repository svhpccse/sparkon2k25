About

Welcome to Sparkon 2025, a state-level technical symposium proudly organized by the Circuit Department of Shree Venkateshwara Hi-Tech Polytechnic College. organized exclusively for diploma students, this event aims to :

  • Encourage students,
  • Inspire learning,
  • Foster collaboration.

Symposium Details

Event Date: 24.01.2025

Venue: SVHPC Campus

Registration Closes On: 21.01.2025

Intimation Date: 22.01.2025

Registration Fee: Rs 100 Per Head (Except Free Fire)

General Instructions

  • Participation Offer:

    Pay ₹100 to participate in any two technical or two non-technical events, but not in one technical and one non-technical event.{except Free Fire : ₹200 per team (4 players) }.

  • College Bus Service:

    All participants can make use of the college bus service on the event day. For more details, visit the symposium website.

  • Arrival Time:

    Participants are required to arrive at the college by 9:00 AM sharp to ensure timely commencement of the event.

  • Registration:

    Please complete your event registration online to ensure smooth participation.

  • Meals & Refreshment:

    Complimentary lunch, tea, and snacks will be provided to all participants during the symposium.

  • ID Card:

    All participants must bring their valid college ID card for identification.

  • Judging Criteria:

    The decision of the judges will be final and binding.

  • Time limit:

    Participants must follow the events timing strictly.

Paper Presentation Rules
  • Teams can have up to 2 members.
  • An abstract of 200–300 words must be submitted within the specified word limit.
  • Ensure your submission is made before the deadline: 21.01.2025.
  • Each team will have 5–8 minutes to present, followed by a 1–2 minute Q&A session.
  • Participants should keep a copy of their presentation on a pendrive or in their email.
  • Participants can deliver their presentations in English and Tamil.
TITLES
  • Blockchain Applications Beyond Cryptocurrencies
  • Green Computing
  • AI In Cloud Computing
  • Blue Eye Technology
Quiz Rules
  • Registration with us is required before the event begins.
  • Preliminary Round: Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
  • Technical Rounds: Rapid-fire questions
Bug Smash Rules
  • Teams can have up to 2 members.
  • You will have 5 minutes to solve each program. A total of 5 programs will be provided.
  • Your program solving time will determine your position in the competition.
Hacking Rules
  • Participants must crack the password using the provided hints and other resources.

Circuit Debugging Rules
  • Participants: Maximum of 2 people per team.
  • Event duration: 30 minutes.
  • The question will be provided at the start of the event, and participants will have 15 minutes to identify the problem.
  • Participants will then have an additional 15 minutes to provide a solution.
Technical Quiz Rules
  • Participants: Maximum of 2 people per team.
  • Event format: Two rounds - preliminary and final.
  • Time limit: Participants must provide their answers within 1 minute.
Best Manager Rules
  • Participants: Individual participation only.
  • Event format: On-the-spot topic selection.
  • Time limit: Participants will have 2 minutes to explain the given topic.

Dance Rules

Mono Acting Rules

About the Institution

Shree Venkateshwara Hi-Tech Polytechnic College, a Seat of Wisdom, is one of the foremost institutions, gifted with a cheerful atmosphere, located at Othakuthirai. The college was established in the year 2009 by Shree Venkateshwara Educational & Charitable Trust. SVHPC has been fortunate to have the guidance of illustrious managing trustees and Principal with foresight and far-reaching vision.

SVHPC has been in the forefront by innovation in technical education. We prepare the students to develop professionalism and censorious thoughts in them. The college offers a haven of peace and tranquil atmosphere for students who have the urge to learn and master. As per the prologue “Survival of the Fittest One”, we earnestly prepare the students to be the fittest in modern society. Discipline and Perfection are the watchwords of our Institution.
